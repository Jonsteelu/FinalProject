OUR MISSION

TEACHING EXCELLENCE AND VIRTUE

At Arete University we strive for excellence and virtue. From giving back to the community to winning international competitions, our students are always working towards their own excellence and virtue. Our facaulty is composed of experienced profesionals, with years worth of experience in their field. We named ourselves after the greek goddess of excellence and virtue, Arete, as this is our primary focus for our students. We aim to prepare them to be excellent and virtuous, both in their carrer fields, as well as in everything else that they do.

OUR HISTORY

We are a university in greece founded in 1893 by proffessor Demetrius Ariti. He created this noble and prestigous institution as a school for teaching students excellence and virtue, firstly in history, greek mythology, and phylosophy. As time progressed our institution expanded our course offerings. Today we offer everything from the liberal arts and the fine arts to computer science and engineering.
